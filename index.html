ProphecyRP

ProphecyRP is a FiveM RP server.

At Prophecy Roleplay, we specialize in creating a fun and unique roleplay experience. We are a custom QBCore economy server full of serious roleplay server with a lot to offer such as whitelisted jobs, whitelisted Police Department, whitelisted EMS and Fire and several others. At Prophecy, we make roleplay enjoyable for all people everyone. It is not too grindy yet not as lax as menu base. There are plenty of businesses for people to open up and run. There are plenty of opportunities to become a kingpin drug lord, a well known criminal, or even a night club owner. There are plenty of gangs and mafias out there to be started as well! You want to gamble or race the city streets of Los Santos? We have that too. The sky is the limit here at Prophecy, we have developers who listen and like taking recommendations for more and new stuff as well! You want something added, come plead your case and we will always listen! We have a very open-minded staff team, development team, and community heads! The community heads, and staff team members are always in voice channels or awaiting for your support.
